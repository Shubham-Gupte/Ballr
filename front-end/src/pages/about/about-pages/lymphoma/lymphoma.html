Lymphoma and Testicular Lymphoma

What is Lymphoma?

Lymphoma is the name for a group of cancers that form when a blood cell (lymphocyte) becomes malignant.
Testicular lymphoma is extranodal, meaning that it is outside of the lymph nodes.

What does that have to do with my balls?

It was found that Doppler flow, or the flow of blood through the vessel measured by ultrasound, to the testes was increased in men with testicular cancer and lymphoma; this means that men with these diseases were found to have higher scrotal temperatures that the average male.
